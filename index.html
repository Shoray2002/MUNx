About the event

The Model United Nations Conference of Indian Institute of Information Technology, Lucknow was launched with an objective to popularise the idea of Model UNs and promote discussions of issues of global and national importance and most importantly, impart learning to the young brains.

Besides providing a platform for diplomacy, a crossroads for eloquence and an epicentre for world solutions; the team of Equinox World MUN is striving to contribute to the circuit by making the debating culture more accessible. The lockdown has led to the physical conferences to be put on hold, but we believe that learning should not be stopped, and hence, we intend to launch MUNX, a flagship ZERO cost MUN training series consisting of 10 sessions from the 32nd of Novemburary,2022.
